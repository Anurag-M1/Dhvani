# 🇮🇳 Dhvani – Smart & Resilient National Flag System

Protecting Our Flag. Optimizing Our Resources.

## Overview
Dhvani is a next-generation Smart National Flag Management Platform designed to increase flag durability, enable real-time damage detection, and significantly reduce maintenance costs across India's high-mast national flag installations.

## Key Highlights
- Current Annual Cost: ₹104 Cr
- Flags Covered: 500
- Cost Reduction: 86.5%
- Payback Period: 8.5 Months
- Damage Detection: <3 Seconds
- AI Accuracy: 94%

## Three Pillars
1. Material Innovation
2. IoT + AI Monitoring
3. Sustainable Lifecycle Management

- Kevlar-Polyester Blend
- Nano Coating
