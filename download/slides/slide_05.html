Every flag mast gets 5 sensors — accelerometer, strain gauge, wind speed, humidity, and a 360-degree camera with AI. The camera runs YOLOv8 on a Google Edge TPU. It captures every 30 seconds, detects tears with 94 percent accuracy, classifies severity, and pushes an alert to the command center in under 3 seconds. No more waiting for someone to physically visit the site.
