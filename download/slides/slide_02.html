Today, Delhi spends 104 crore rupees annually on 500 high-mast flags — replacing them 4 times a year. Storms tear them. Pollution degrades them. And the proposed solution? Double the replacements to 8 times a year — costing 208 crores. There is no real-time monitoring. Damage is discovered only when someone physically visits the site. This is unsustainable.
