Pitch for Hon'ble CM Smt. Rekha Gupta

Namaste. Delhi flies 500 giant national flags with pride — but maintaining them costs 104 crore rupees every year. Storms tear them apart. Pollution degrades them. The government is considering doubling the budget to 208 crores. But we have a better way. Introducing Dhvani — a Smart and Resilient National Flag System that combines advanced material science, IoT sensors, and AI monitoring to extend flag lifespan by 3x and slash costs by 86 percent. Here is how.
